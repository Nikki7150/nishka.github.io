  • Many immigrant and first gen students struggle to adapt to the US system.
  • Challenges include language barriers, cultural differences and academic pressure.
  • This cause support them with resources, guidance and emotional support.
  • The goal is to help students succeed in school, build confidence, and prepare for college.
  • Sharing personal stories and tips builds a sense of community and belonging.
